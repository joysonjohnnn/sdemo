Welcome to The Muscle Theraphy

Massage therapy can be used for the treatment of both acute and chronic conditions. I work with a wide variety of clients, of all ages, in the treatment of illness, injury rehabilitation and disability and general well-being. Massage therapy is as vital to a healthy mind and body as is exercise and diet. All too often, people who have demanding lives turn to temporary solutions that do not necessarily promote healing or wellness. There is a better choice, I invite you to discover the benefits of massage therapy.

I specialise in Soft Tissue Therapy (Sports injury specific) and I am qualified to the UK's highest level 5 BTEC Diploma in Clinical Sports and Remedial Massage Therapy, studied through the Oxford School of Sports Massage and recently qualified in Manual Lymphhatic Drainage through Lymphoedema Training Academy. I have over 13 years of experience and have a member’s license with the Federation of Holistic Therapies. (FHT Members number 189453)
