Mandar Sakpal (SPORTS MYTHERAPIST)

Clinical Sports and Remedial Soft Tissue Therapist & LTA Manual Lymphatic Drainage Therapist (Fill & Flush)

In 2009 I obtained my level 5 BTEC Diploma in Clinical Sports and Remedial Massage which is the current highest level in the UK. I studied at the Oxford School of Sports Massage specializing in Sports and Remedial Massage Therapy, Injury Treatment, Prevention and Rehabilitation. There are only 12 institutions in the UK that provide this level of training and only therapists qualified to level 4 and above are considered to be highly qualified in this field. Like myself, I can then become a member of the Sports Massage Association and Institute of Remedial Massage Therapist.

I am fully insured and have a gold members license with the SMA, Sports Massage Association. The Sports Massage Association are the UK and Ireland's largest professional association for Sports Soft Tissue Therapists. Becoming a full member of SMA means I continually renew my qualifications on an annual basis and also SMA members are expected to abide by the SMAs Code of Conduct of Professional Practice that enables members to have a common guide to excellence in professional conduct and practice, and to meet and even exceed the expectations of clients.

I have worked at many local sporting event's providing pre, intermediate and post massage to participating sports men and women. I have also taken part in national sporting event's such as 'Sleepless in the Saddle' which is a 24 hour cycling event, providing pre and post sports massage to athlete's throughout the night and also the London marathon.
